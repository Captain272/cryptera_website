DEMOSTRATOR OF THE DEPARTMENT

The student have a chance to present their thought provoking thoughts on the occasion of engineers day, it is an immense opportunity for a student to expose the inner engineer. Student have a choice to choose their own topic related to the respective department or other categories also.

Rounds :

1. First round Student should send the abstract related to their topic before Sept 11th evening 6:00AM. Abstract should cover the introduction, uses or applications, brief view and conclusion about topic. Selected students have a chance to present the presentation. 2. Second round Students those who are selected for the first round. Should present their thoughts in the google meet. Student have 8minutes time to present their thoughts through PPT(with not exceeding 15 slides)
